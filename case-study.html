Overview

NovaShop is a direct-to-consumer fashion brand that had outgrown its off-the-shelf store. They needed a bespoke platform that matched their brand, loaded instantly, and gave their small team full control over products and orders — without paying per-transaction fees to a SaaS provider.

I led the project from the first wireframe to production deployment: research, UI/UX design, frontend, backend, and the admin tooling behind it.

The challenge

The previous store was slow, generic, and clunky to manage. Cart abandonment was high, and the team spent hours each week fighting their admin panel instead of selling.

"We were losing customers at checkout and drowning in manual work. We needed something fast, beautiful, and ours."

The solution

I designed a clean, conversion-focused storefront with a streamlined three-step checkout and Stripe integration. On the backend, a custom Node.js API with PostgreSQL powers products, inventory, and orders, while a purpose-built admin dashboard makes day-to-day management effortless.
